Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective date: June 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data Āyurveda Food Compass collects, how it is used, and how you can control it.

1. Who We Are

Āyurveda Food Compass ("the App", "we", "us") is operated by Kerez Law / Igor Kerez. The App is accessible at ayurveda-food.com and through mobile applications.

Contact: privacy@kerezlaw.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Account data — email address and name when you register or sign in with Google.
  • Dosha profile — your Ayurvedic constitution (Vāta, Pitta, or Kapha) as determined by the in-app assessment.
  • Food journal — names and Ayurvedic evaluation results of foods you analyse in the App.
  • Food photos — images you upload for AI analysis. Photos are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active subscription, trial, or expired plan.
  • Usage data — basic technical information (device type, browser, country) collected automatically for service improvement.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise the Ayurvedic food evaluation service.
  • To manage your account and subscription.
  • To sync your food journal across devices.
  • To process payments securely via Stripe.
  • To improve the App based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. AI Food Analysis

When you analyse food using the camera or text input, your query (text or image) is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API for processing. This is necessary to provide the Ayurvedic evaluation.

  • Food images are transmitted securely and are not retained after analysis.
  • Anthropic may process data in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.
  • Analysis results (text only) are stored in your food journal in our database.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (EU servers). Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing. We never store your card details. Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in with Google. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered food analysis. Privacy Policy
  • Netlify — hosting and serverless functions. Privacy Policy

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase databases located in the European Union (Ireland, eu-west-1). All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

Access to your data is protected by Row Level Security — only you can access your own account data.

7. Data Retention

  • Account and food journal data is retained while your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Food photos are never stored — they are deleted immediately after analysis.
  • Payment records are retained as required by applicable law (typically 7 years).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction — update inaccurate data via your account settings.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — request your food journal data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent — cancel your subscription and stop using the App at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@kerezlaw.com.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

10. Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses browser local storage (not cookies) to save your preferences, dosha profile, and food journal locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is also synced to your account in our database when you are signed in.

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

11. International Users

The App is available worldwide. If you access the App from outside the European Union, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in the EU. By using the App you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:
